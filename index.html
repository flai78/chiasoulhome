The House

A Sardinian Soul in Every Stone

Nestled in the hills above Chia, one of Sardinia's most beloved coastal villages, this authentic stone house combines rustic charm with all the comforts you need for an unforgettable holiday.

Exposed wooden beams, a traditional fireplace framed by a hand-laid stone wall, terracotta floors and handcrafted ceramics tell the story of a home built with love — and shared with warmth.

From the panoramic terraces and the lush pergola-covered garden you can breathe in the Sardinian landscape: rolling hills, Mediterranean scrub and, on clear days, the shimmering blue of the sea on the horizon.

Location

Chia, South Sardinia

Chia is one of Sardinia's most treasured coastal destinations — a small village blessed with dunes, lagoons, Phoenician towers and crystalline waters in impossibly vivid shades of turquoise.

The house sits on a hillside just minutes from the coast, offering the perfect balance between nature, privacy and access to all the best the south of the island has to offer.

  • 5 min to Chia beaches (Su Giudeu, Cala Cipolla, Tuerredda)
  • 35 min to Cagliari city centre
  • 40 min to Cagliari Elmas Airport (CAG)
  • Close to restaurants, supermarkets & village life
